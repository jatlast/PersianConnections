If you live outside of the United States and do not have a credit card that is valid for purchases within the United States...

 

You should be able to purchase an American Cashiers Check or a valid American Money Order from an international bank in your area.

Please make sure that:

  • The check or money order is in U.S. Dollars and made payable to Personals Connections.
  • You include your Connections user name.
  • You include your email address so that we are able to contact you if necessary.
  • The amount is: 1 month ($9.99), 3 months ($24.99) or 12 months ($94.99).
  • Mail to:
  Persian Connections
  54 Baker Road
  Livingston, NJ
  07039
  USA
 

 

Also - You may have a friend or relative who has a valid credit card for purchases within the United States that would be willing to let you use their credit card to become a member. If so, that may be fastest and easiest way for you to become a member.
